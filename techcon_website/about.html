Our History

TechCon began as a small meetup of engineers and designers who wanted to share practical knowledge. Over the years it has grown into an annual conference bringing together professionals and students worldwide.

Each year we focus on hands-on workshops, inclusive panels, and new tools that help teams build better products.

Our Mission

Our mission is to connect builders, thinkers, and leaders so they can learn from each other and accelerate technology that benefits everyone.

We value accessibility, practical education, and community-driven innovation.
